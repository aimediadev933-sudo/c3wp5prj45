About MEITA

MEITA integrates IoT, robotics, and digital technologies to monitor and sustainably manage mangrove and aquatic ecosystems. Our project supports environmental education, real-time monitoring, and community engagement for coastal sustainability.

We address critical challenges including biodiversity threats, pollution, and unsustainable practices while strengthening regional collaborations across Asia-Pacific and beyond.

Our Mission

To utilize IoT, community engagement, and interdisciplinary research to manage aquatic and mangrove ecosystems sustainably and promote environmental awareness through innovative technology solutions.
